What I do

Music that matters —
from start to finish

I'm Joel Banz — musician, music producer and audio engineer from Luzern. Under the name Nährhaft Studio, I guide artists through the entire creative process: from composition and recording to the finished, release-ready master.

01

Composition

Together we develop musical ideas, arrangements and structures — whether building from scratch or evolving your existing concepts.

02

Production

Professional recording with first-class equipment. I create the sound and atmosphere your music deserves.

03

Mixing

Every track finds its place. I shape the sound into a coherent, balanced whole — with depth, dynamics and character.

04

Mastering

The final touch. I optimise loudness, tone and dynamics for all platforms — from streaming to vinyl.

Creative Work

Composition & Sound

Alongside studio work, independent artistic projects take shape — from stage music to electronic soundscapes.

Theatre & Classical

Compositions for theatre productions and classical projects — music that creates space, carries atmosphere and brings stories to life.

Sound Design

Using an arsenal of modular and analogue synthesizers to build atmospheric soundscapes and experimental sonic worlds beyond conventional structures.

Nährwerk

Electronic music under the artist name Nährwerk — an independent musical voice built from synthesis, rhythm and silence.
